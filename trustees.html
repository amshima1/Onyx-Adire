Onyx-Adire was born from a singular obsession: to prove that ancient Yoruba craftsmanship belongs on the world’s most prestigious runways. Our founder envisions a world where Adire is not just a fabric, but a global language of luxury. By marrying the soulful, hand-dyed artistry of our ancestors with sophisticated silhouettes, we are creating a new legacy.
